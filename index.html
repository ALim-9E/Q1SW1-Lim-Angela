I have watched a few animes, but this one is the one that stuck with me.

Detective Conan is an anime about a 17 year old detective, known as Shinichi Kudo, has been shruken to his 7 year old body by a mysterious organization, now living under the pseudonym as Conan Edogawa, his goal is to bring back his life and bring his perpetrators to justice.

Why I love Detective Conan

  • Characters are adorable!
  • Action scenes are very exciting
  • Relationships of each character to others

Main Characters of Detective Conan

  1. Shinichi Kudo: Highschool detective who got shrunk into a kid
  2. Ran Mouri: Childhood friend of Shinichi
  3. Kogoro Mouri: Father of Ran Mouri and a private detective

I am very happy that I decided to invest in this anime, for I have never felt such great joy watching the characters bring justice to light!
